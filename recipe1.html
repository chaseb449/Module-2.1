California Burrito Recipe
Ingredients Directions
Tortilla Lay tortilla open
Thinly sliced Steak Cook steak, and then put in a line on tortilla
Chorizo Cook chorizo then spread on top of the steak in the tortilla
Rice Cook rice and then spread out on tortilla
Beans Cook beans and spread on tortilla with the rice
Lettuce Sprinkle lettuce on tortilla
Melted Cheese Roll up tortilla and spread hot melted cheese on top
